# A/HRC/43/15 - Report of the Working Group on the Universal Periodic Review: Slovenia

**Document:** A/HRC/43/15  
**Date:** 26 December 2019  
**Session:** Human Rights Council, Forty-third session  
**Agenda item 6:** Universal Periodic Review  

---

## I. Summary of the proceedings of the review process

### A. Presentation by the State under review

1. The delegation of Slovenia was headed by the State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ms. Simona Drenik. The delegation included representatives of the Ministry of Justice, Ministry of the Interior, Ministry of Labour, Family, Social Affairs and Equal Opportunities, Ministry of Education, Science and Sport, and the Government Office for National Minorities.

2. Slovenia presented its national human rights priorities and achievements since the second UPR cycle. Slovenia highlighted that the Human Rights Ombudsman had obtained "A" status accreditation with the Global Alliance of National Human Rights Institutions (GANHRI) in 2017.

3. Slovenia reported on the implementation of the National Programme of Measures for Roma for the period 2017-2021, focusing on education, employment, housing, health care, and the preservation of Roma culture and language.

4. Regarding the "erased" persons, Slovenia informed that the compensation scheme had been established and proceedings were continuing.

5. Slovenia presented measures taken to combat hate speech and hate crimes, including amendments to the Criminal Code and training for law enforcement officials.

## III. Follow-up

125. Slovenia requested that the recommendations in paragraphs 123.38, 123.39, 123.40 and 124.1 to 124.3 be considered as "noted" pending further consideration.
